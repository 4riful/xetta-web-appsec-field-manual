## Non-Merge Decisions

- Broad resource collections such as cheat sheets, blogs, writeups, and videos were grouped by resource type, not merged into vulnerability notes, because their primary function is reference discovery.
- Raw source files were preserved separately to guarantee recovery if a future maintainer wants to re-split content.
- Stub notes were merged only when their topic was clear from title and links.
