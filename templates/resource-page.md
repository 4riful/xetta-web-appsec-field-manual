# Resource Page

## Purpose

Explain what this resource group helps the reader do. Keep the goal practical: learn a topic, find tools, compare writeups, choose payload references, or follow a workflow.

## Fields

- Audience: beginner, hunter, pentester, developer, maintainer.
- Resource types: tools, writeups, cheat sheets, videos, payloads, labs, or mixed.
- Best starting point: one or two links that should be read first.
- Related bug classes: where the resource applies.
- Related payloads/tools: where to go next.

## Notes

- Do not create a raw link dump when a short explanation would help.
- Keep noisy material in database views, not top-level curated lists.
- If a resource is old, dangerous, or narrow, say that directly.
