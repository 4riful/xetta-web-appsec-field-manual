# One-Liners

## Standard Format

Every one-liner must include:

- Purpose.
- Required tools.
- Input.
- Command.
- Output.
- Risk/noise.
- When not to use.

## Why

Opaque one-liners are hard to trust. A star-worthy manual explains what the command does and how to review output.
